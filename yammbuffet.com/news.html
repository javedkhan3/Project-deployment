New buffet Service during Covid19

 

During these hard times,  from19th August 2020 we have been instructed by the Government Guidelines that only TABLE SERVICE will be allowed. What that means is that all customers will have to order food directly from their tables by scanning the QR CODE rather than queuing up in the line .

We apologize for any inconvenience caused. 

Thank you so much for your cooperation! STAY SAFE!

Yamm Buffet Team

  • Temperature check on arrival, should not over 37.8c
  • We will limit the number of people at the buffet at each time, with social distancing markers throughout the buffet in a one-way system. 
  • For the customer who has an exemption of using face covering, table service is available. 
  • Upon arrival, the customer’s name, contact number, and arriving time will be taken and kept for 21 days to help with the NHS Test and Trace.
  • Cashiers will be behind the protective screens.

STAFF & TRAINING

All our staff are wearing PPE and have undergone bespoke Covid-19 related training modules, in-depth risk assessment training, and health and safety refreshers upon their return to work. 

HEALTH & SAFETY

 We have always had the highest standards to ensure cleanliness throughout the Restaurant. All areas, doors, chairs etc. are disinfected after every use, with every table being fully sanitized between bookings. The venues will receive a full deep clean on the days we’re closed. Hand sanitiser will be at all entrances and exits available for everyone’s use.
